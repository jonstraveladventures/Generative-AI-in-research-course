## Citation corrections made during this week's collection

While verifying each arXiv ID against the lesson HTML, two errors were found and corrected in `docs/week-3/Infrastructure Scale and the Rebound Problem.html` (and the corresponding source files):

1. **Gupta et al. "Chasing Carbon"** — was linked to `arXiv:2010.06650` (a math paper on Fourier-Stieltjes algebras). The correct ID is `arXiv:2011.02839`.
2. **"Efficiency is Not Enough"** — was attributed to "Tamburrino et al." with title "A Critical Perspective on Sustainable AI". The correct authors are Wright, Igel, Samuel & Selvan and the correct subtitle is "A Critical Perspective **of Environmentally** Sustainable AI".
