## Citation correction noted (to be fixed)

Sub-lesson 6.1 (`Writing as Thinking.html`) currently embeds the link "[Your Brain on ChatGPT](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12723506/)" inside the sentence describing the MIT Media Lab study and its findings (54 participants, lowest brain engagement, reduced alpha/beta connectivity). That PMC ID is a one-page BJGP commentary by Armitage citing the study, not the study itself. The actual study is Kosmyna et al. on arXiv ([2506.08872](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.08872)). The lesson link should point there.
